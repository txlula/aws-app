Daily Mood Checker

It is time to track your daily mood and feelings.

Welcome! 😊 This form only takes a couple of minutes to fill in. Just follow the steps below, working from top to bottom.
1. Choose your mood. 🙋‍♀️
Look for the three mood buttons near the top of the page — Happy 🙂, Neutral 😐, and Sad 😞. Click the one that best matches how you're feeling right now. You'll notice the face image on the page changes to match your choice, so you can see it's been registered.
2. Explain your mood. 🤔
In the box that says "What is the reason for your mood?", type a short sentence or two about why you're feeling that way today. For example: "I had a great lunch with a friend" or "I'm feeling a bit tired after a busy week."
3. Set your energy level. 🔥
You'll see a slider bar with the label "Energy Level" and a number beside it (it starts at 50 out of 100). Click and drag the slider to the left if your energy is low, or to the right if you're feeling energetic. The number will update as you move it, so you can see your score.
4. Share something you're grateful for. 🙏
In the box labelled "What is something you are grateful for today?", write down one thing you appreciate — it can be something big or something small, like good weather, a kind word, or a hot cup of tea.
5. Set a goal for tomorrow. 🎯
In the box labelled "What is something you want to accomplish tomorrow?", write one thing you'd like to do or achieve the next day. It doesn't have to be ambitious — even something simple like "go for a short walk" counts!
6. Add any extra thoughts (optional). 💭
If there's anything else on your mind, you can type it in the final box labelled "Any additional ideas or thoughts?" This field is completely optional, so feel free to leave it blank if you have nothing to add.
7. Submit your form. ✅
Once you're happy with your answers, click the Submit button at the bottom of the form.
What happens next? After you click Submit, your answers will appear in a Submission Results section that shows up at the bottom of the page. Take a moment to look over what you've entered. When you're done, click the Close button to dismiss the results.
That's it — you're all done! 🎉
